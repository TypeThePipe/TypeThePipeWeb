Are you considering to check in a Datathon or data competition? In this post we’ll present 10 tips that can help you to outperform your rivals. So without further ado, let’s get started.


1. Set your main goal and check your time and resources(skills, hardware…)

2. Choose the challenge field/industry according your interests

3. Build the right team. Set up tools to exchange code.

4. Deep research about the challenge topic and environment.

5. Excel your exploratory analysis. Remember you could use external data sources.

6. Set your project and code structure.

7. Run your models.

8. Cross Validation, OOS and backtesting.

9. Model interpretability

10. Write down your observed strengths and weaknesses. It will be the starting point for your next proyect.

  • 6. Establece la estructura del proyecto y del código.
  • 7. Lanza los primeros modelos.
  • 8. Validación cruzada, OOS y backtesting.
  • 9. Interpretación y evaluación de resultados desde el punto de vista práctico y/o negocio.
  • 10. Escribe una memoria con puntos fuertes y puntos a mejorar detectados, que te sirvan de partida en el próximo reto.
